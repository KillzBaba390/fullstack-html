The Best Movies According To Seun

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Coming To America

A classic comedy about an African prince who goes undercover to find true love in New York City.

The Chronicles Of Narnia: The Lion, The Witch And The Wardrobe

An epic fantasy film based on the beloved novel by C.S. Lewis, featuring a magical world and a battle between good and evil.

The Shawshank Redemption

A powerful drama about hope and friendship, set in a prison and based on a novella by Stephen King.
